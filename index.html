6DOF Engine

We build a real-time tracking engine that combines multiple sensor sources into one stable 6DOF signal. Our goal is smooth motion, low drift, and reliable positioning for everyday VR sessions and experimental setups.

SteamVR Bridge

OpenDriver VR acts as a bridge between custom tracking hardware and SteamVR runtime. That means your devices can be recognized in a familiar ecosystem without building everything from scratch.

DIY Hardware

We support makers, tinkerers, and prototype builders using Kinect, webcams, and custom rigs. The project is open-source, community-driven, and focused on making VR tracking more accessible to everyone.
